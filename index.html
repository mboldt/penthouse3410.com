Penthouse at The Pointe of St. Paul

Unit 3305 · 78 E 10th Street, Saint Paul, MN 55101

The Highest Address in Downtown St. Paul

Perched on the 33rd floor of The Pointe — the tallest residential building in Saint Paul — this two-bedroom corner penthouse delivers something money rarely buys: true panoramic views in every direction. To the north, the gold dome of the Minnesota State Capitol glows against the sky. To the west, the Minneapolis skyline rises above the tree line. To the east and south, the Mississippi River and Pedro Park spread out below you. This is the top of the city, and it shows.

Inside the Unit

This corner unit was designed to live as well as it looks. The open layout connects the living and dining areas seamlessly, drawing natural light from multiple exposures throughout the day. Recent upgrades include upgraded lighting, newer woodwork, and wood-look tile floors throughout. The kitchen is open and functional, and the in-unit washer and dryer means no trips to a shared laundry room.

Location

The Pointe sits at the corner of Minnesota Avenue and 10th Street in the heart of downtown Saint Paul. Everything the city offers is steps away: the St. Paul Farmers Market, Xcel Energy Center, the Ordway Center for the Performing Arts, the Fitzgerald Theater, Rice Park, the Minnesota Children's Museum, and a wide range of restaurants along St. Peter Street. The Green Line light rail stop at 10th Street is just two blocks away, connecting you directly to Minneapolis. Pedro Park, the newly renovated neighborhood park across the street, offers shaded gathering areas, a pavilion, a playground, and a dog area.

Interstates 94 and 35E are minutes away by car, and Minneapolis–St. Paul International Airport is roughly 15 minutes from your door. A Lunds & Byerlys grocery store is right across the street.
